Help: Adding the URL

  • Firefox
  • Chrome

Firefox

Follow these steps to add the URL in Firefox:

  1. Click on the menu button and select Preferences.
  2. Go to the Search section.
  3. Click on Add search engine.
  4. Enter the URL and click Add.

Chrome

Follow these steps to add the URL in Chrome:

  1. Click on the menu button and select Settings.
  2. Go to the Search engine section.
  3. Click on Manage search engines.
  4. Click on Add and enter the URL.
